ABOUT RECALLQ

Built in Australia. By two founders who were tired of watching practices lose patients.

RecallQ was co-founded in 2026 after we kept hearing the same complaint from practice owners: "We know who's overdue. We just can't get to them."

We spent months talking to practice owners across dental, physio, and optometry. The same story everywhere: the patient data was sitting unused in a perfectly good PMS, revenue quietly leaking out the back door.

Existing tools didn't solve it. US platforms without AU compliance. Generic SMS tools requiring technical setup. Practice management software that tracks recalls but never sends them.

So we built a master platform with sub-brands. A dental recall isn't a physio review isn't an optometry check. The language, timing, and context all differ. One engine, three voices.

Australian matters. Privacy Act, Spam Act, AHPRA, health rebate cycles, AU SMS gateways. You can't retrofit a US product for this — you have to build it here, from scratch, with healthcare compliance as the foundation.

Our commitment: healthcare-grade compliance, ROI in month one, and deep vertical expertise in the three practices we serve. Nothing more. Nothing startup-flashy.
