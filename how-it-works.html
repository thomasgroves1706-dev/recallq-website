HOW IT WORKS

From CSV upload to confirmed bookings — in under 20 minutes.

Here's exactly what happens — whether you run a dental, physio, or optometry practice.

1

Upload

Export from your practice management system. CSV with patient name, mobile, last visit, treatment. Auto-detects columns, validates AU mobile formats, flags duplicates.

2

Set rules

Choose recall windows by treatment type. Set your send window (default 9am–6pm local, never Sundays) and tone.

3

AI personalises each message

RecallQ reads each patient's context and writes a unique SMS in your practice voice. Not a mail merge.

4

Reminders go out automatically

Overnight scheduling. You wake up to a dashboard showing what went out and what's coming.

5

Replies are classified instantly

YES books. NO releases the slot. Ambiguous replies are read and routed. STOP is honoured forever.

6

Bookings land in your calendar

Calendly for MVP. You see recovered revenue on your dashboard.
